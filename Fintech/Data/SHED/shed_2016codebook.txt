----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------CODEBOOK FOR 2016 SURVEY OF HOUSEHOLD ECONOMICS AND DECISIONMAKING
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------The codebook serves as the principal guide to the variables included on the
public version of the 2016 Survey of Household Economics and Decisionmaking
(SHED) data set.
The SHED is sponsored by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System.
Data for the 2016 SHED were collected by GfK using their online probability
based KnowledgePanel.
For a general overview of the 2015 SHED and a detailed discussion of the survey
methods, see Jeff Larrimore, Alex Durante, Christina Park, and Anna Tranfaglia,
"Report on the Economic Well-Being of U.S. Households in 2016," Federal Reserve
Board.
The variables in the codebook are generally in the order in which the questions
were asked during the survey. For a copy of the survey questionaire, see
"Appendix 1" of "Report on the Economic Well-Being of U.S. Households in 2016."
